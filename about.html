About This Project

Khaos: A Choose Your Own Adventure is an interactive narrative set in a realm beyond life and death. You take the role of Azariel Dalvir, a "reaper" tasked with judging souls. Heaven or hell...those are the choices you're given.

But what happens when duty turns into doubt? When repetition starts to blur your sense of purpose? As you progress through the story, the lines between what’s right, what’s real, and what’s yours begin to fade.

Your decisions will shape not just the fates of others, but also the story of Azariel themselves. There are no wrong choices... only consequences.

What to Expect

  • Multi-path branching choices
  • Moral dilemmas: duty vs freedom
  • Soul profiles with unique backstories
  • Existential crisis (and some emotional damage)

How to Play

Scroll down or tap anywhere on the start screen to begin your adventure. You’ll be presented with profiles of souls: read their stories and decide their fate. After judging all souls, you’ll be faced with a personal choice. Choose wisely...Or don’t. :)
